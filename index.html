"End of an era, rest in melody": "Tributes for Lata Mangeshkar"

As the mellifluous voice of Indias nightingale Lata Mangeshkar fell silent on 6 feb, tributes poured in for the legendary singer.Mangeshkar, one of the biggest music icons of the country, and recipient of Bharat Ratna, Her contribution to Indian music industry in a career spanning seven decades gained her honorific titles such as the Nightingale of India.She recorded songs in over thirty-six Indian languages and a few foreign languages.She is widely considered as one of the greatest and most influential singers in India.
